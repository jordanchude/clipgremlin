Powerful Features for Smart Streamers

ClipGremlin combines cutting-edge AI technology with Twitch expertise to create the most intelligent chat engagement bot available.

Real-time Audio Processing

ClipGremlin captures your live stream audio using low-latency HLS processing, converting it to optimized chunks for lightning-fast transcription.

  • ~300ms processing latency
  • 16kHz mono audio optimization
  • Automatic quality adjustment
  • FFmpeg-powered reliability

AI-Powered Transcription

Using OpenAI's Whisper-Turbo, ClipGremlin understands what you're saying in real-time with incredible accuracy across multiple languages.

  • 25+ languages supported
  • Automatic language detection
  • Context-aware processing
  • Noise filtering built-in

Contextual Prompt Generation

GPT-3.5-Turbo analyzes your recent stream content to generate perfectly timed, relevant prompts that spark genuine conversation.

  • Content-aware prompts
  • PG-13 and ToS-safe
  • Mischievous but friendly tone
  • Multi-language responses

Intelligent Silence Detection

Advanced algorithms monitor your chat activity, detecting the perfect moments to engage without overwhelming your audience.

  • Configurable silence duration
  • Rolling window analysis
  • Smart timing optimization
  • No spam or flooding
